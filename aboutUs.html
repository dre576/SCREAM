The creators of SCREAM!:

Andrea Johnson (left) and Nicole Hermoso (right) are computer science students at the University of Montana. Their hobbies include eating, doing homework and loving their pets. SCREAM! was created after their many failed attempts of finding a food establishment to eat. Since they are smart computer scientists, SCREAM! was born and their search for the perfect place to eat was solved.
